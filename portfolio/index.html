The Work

Visual systems and storytelling assets that help ideas become easier to understand, share, and return to.

Craft & Being supports the translation between a brand’s interior world and the places it shows up online. This portfolio gathers samples of social design, workbook and resource layouts, visual direction, logo explorations, and campaign assets created to give each body of work a more thoughtful public form.

Each engagement is shaped around the message, audience, and season of the brand, with attention to rhythm, clarity, and restraint.
